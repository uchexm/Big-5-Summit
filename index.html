BIG 5 SUMMIT

The Big Five refer to African lions, leopards, rhinoceros and Cape buffalo. Although they are very dangerous animals they are not invincible and the African lion, leopard and elephant are all classed as ‘vulnerable’ by the International Union for the Conservation of Nature. We invite you to thie epic event taking place in Cape Town South Africa on the 5th of January 2024.
